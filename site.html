GamesDotChat

Welcome to GamesDotChat where we give you an extra level of fun on your Twitch livestreams, with games your chat viewers can play games with each other.

Connect 4

Connect 4 uses the global VoteYea and VoteNay emotes to place disc into the board slots. It's easy to add this game to your livestream.

1) Add the following web browser source to your layout.
https://connect4.games.chat/your-channel-name

2) Make GamesDotChat a moderator in your chat, so it may give instructions in every chat mode.
